**PROMPT — CLIENT PROFITABILITY BREAKDOWN TAB**

Add a Profitability tab to every client profile in Qleno. This applies to both residential and commercial clients. The tab shows a full profitability breakdown with time filters and is used by office staff to make rate increase decisions. This is a read-only analytics view — no editing.

---

**STEP 1 — DATABASE**

No new tables needed. All data is derived from existing tables:
- Revenue → `job_history` and `jobs` tables (completed jobs for this client)
- Labor cost → `job_technicians.final_pay` summed across all jobs for this client
- Materials/supplies → add a `supply_cost` column to `jobs` if it does not exist: `ALTER TABLE jobs ADD COLUMN IF NOT EXISTS supply_cost numeric(8,2) DEFAULT 0.00;` — this allows office to log supply cost per job in the future; default to 0 for now
- Overhead → calculated as a fixed percentage of revenue (default 10% — configurable in Company Settings)
- Net profit → Revenue - Labor - Materials - Overhead

Add an overhead rate setting to Company Settings if it does not exist:
```sql
INSERT INTO company_settings (company_id, key, value)
VALUES (company_id, 'overhead_rate_pct', '10')
ON CONFLICT DO NOTHING;
```

---

**STEP 2 — PROFITABILITY TAB ON CLIENT PROFILE**

Add a **Profitability** tab to the client profile page. Position it after the existing tabs (Jobs, Invoices, etc.).

The tab has three sections stacked vertically:

**Section A — KPI Strip (top)**

Four metric tiles in a row:

```
Monthly Revenue    YTD Revenue    Total Jobs    Avg Bill
$X,XXX             $XX,XXX        XX            $XXX
```

All four update based on the selected time filter.

**Section B — Time Filter**

Three pill buttons: **Monthly** | **Quarterly** | **Annually**

Default to Monthly. When a filter is selected, all data below recalculates for that period. Monthly = last 30 days. Quarterly = last 90 days. Annually = last 365 days.

**Section C — Revenue Trend Chart**

Line chart showing revenue over the selected period. X-axis = time (weeks for monthly, months for quarterly/annually). Y-axis = revenue in dollars. Use Phes brand color `#5B9BD5` for the line. Clean minimal chart — no grid clutter.

**Section D — Profitability Breakdown**

A breakdown table matching the Evinco CRM format:

```
PROFITABILITY BREAKDOWN

Revenue              $X,XXX/mo     100%    [full width bar in #5B9BD5]
Labor cost           $X,XXX/mo      XX%    [bar in #E53E3E if >40%, #F6AD55 if 35-40%, #48BB78 if <35%]
Materials/supplies   $XXX/mo         X%    [bar in #5B9BD5 muted]
Overhead allocation  $XXX/mo        10%    [bar in #5B9BD5 muted]
Net profit           $X,XXX/mo      XX%    [bar in #48BB78 if positive, #E53E3E if negative]
```

Each row shows: label, dollar amount per period (normalized to monthly equivalent regardless of filter), percentage of revenue, and a horizontal bar proportional to the percentage.

Labor cost calculation: sum of `job_technicians.final_pay` for all completed jobs for this client in the selected period.
Materials: sum of `jobs.supply_cost` for this client in the selected period.
Overhead: revenue × overhead_rate_pct from company settings.
Net profit: Revenue - Labor - Materials - Overhead.

**Section E — Account Health Score**

A circular gauge (donut chart) in the top right of the profitability breakdown section showing a score from 0–100.

Score calculation:
- Start at 100
- Deduct 20 points if labor cost % > 40%
- Deduct 10 points if labor cost % > 35%
- Deduct 15 points if net profit % < 15%
- Deduct 10 points if net profit % < 20%
- Deduct 10 points if client has missed or cancelled more than 2 jobs in the period
- Deduct 10 points if client has no completed job in the last 45 days
- Deduct 5 points if avg bill is below the company average avg bill

Color the gauge:
- 75–100: green (`#48BB78`)
- 50–74: yellow (`#F6AD55`)
- 0–49: red (`#E53E3E`)

Show the numeric score inside the gauge and a label below: "Account Health"

**Section F — Top Services by Revenue**

A simple ranked list of service types for this client, showing what percentage of their revenue comes from each:

```
TOP SERVICES BY REVENUE

Deep Clean                    ████████████  65%
One-Time Standard Clean       ████          18%
Recurring Maintenance         ██            10%
Move In / Move Out            █              7%
```

Use horizontal bars in `#5B9BD5` at varying opacity. Show top 5 services maximum.

**STEP 3 — RATE INCREASE INDICATOR**

At the top of the Profitability tab, show a banner if the account health score is below 60 or if net profit % is below 15%:

```
[yellow warning icon] This client may be a candidate for a rate increase. 
Net profit is XX% — below the healthy threshold of 20%.
```

Use background `#FFFBEB`, border `#F6AD55`, text `#92400E`. This is informational only — no action button needed yet (Rate Increase Tool is a future sprint).

---

**STEP 4 — PERMISSIONS**

The Profitability tab is visible to Owner and Office roles only. Never visible to technicians or clients.

---

**STEP 5 — COMPANY SETTINGS — OVERHEAD RATE**

In Company Settings → under a new "Financials" section or existing General tab, add one field:

```
Overhead Rate:  [10]%
(Applied to all client profitability calculations)
```

Owner can adjust this. Changing it recalculates all profitability scores immediately.
