Great question — and this is a critical distinction for running your SaaS business. You currently have **two completely separate views** that need to exist:

---

**What you're in right now:**
The **Tenant Portal** — the owner dashboard for PHES Cleaning LLC specifically. Every cleaning company that signs up gets this exact same interface, branded with their own colors and logo. This is the product you're selling.

---

**What you also need:**
The **Super Admin Portal** — this is YOUR view as the platform owner. Think of it like the god mode. You see every company on the platform, their subscription status, their usage, their billing, and you can jump into any tenant's account.

---

**It needs two separate things:**

**1. A separate login or role switch**

When you log in as `owner@phescleaning.com` you're a tenant owner. You need a separate super admin account — something like `sal@[yourappname].com` with the `super_admin` role in the database. That account gets a completely different dashboard.

**2. A Super Admin Dashboard at `/admin`**

---

**The "View as Tenant" feature (impersonation) is critical for you:**

When a tenant calls you with a problem, you click "View as Tenant" next to their company name and you're instantly inside their portal seeing exactly what they see — their data, their brand colors, their employees. You fix the issue and click "Exit to Admin" to come back. This is how MaidCentral's support team works, how every SaaS support team works.
